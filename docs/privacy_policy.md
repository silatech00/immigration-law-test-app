# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

**Immigration Law AI** ("we", "our", "the service") provides general immigration information through a web chat interface.

## What we collect

We do not collect personal data. The chat interface is provided for informational purposes only and we do not retain user information.

## How we use information

We do not use automated decision-making. All responses are general informational content.

## Data storage

We do not store chat messages, user profiles, or eligibility assessments. Sessions are ephemeral and nothing is written to disk.

## Third parties

We do not share data with third parties.

## Your rights

Because we do not process personal data, standard GDPR data subject rights (access, deletion, portability) do not apply.
